[RAW LYRICS]
[Hook]
I trace the shadows falling on your face
We are standing in a quiet kind of grace
My hands are pulling you a little near
There is nothing but the two of us right here

[Verse 1]
The midnight wind is blowing through the door
I never knew what I was looking for
Until you laid your head upon my chest
And gave my wandering a place to rest

[Pre-Chorus]
Every single breath is pulling me so close
You are the only magic that I need the most
I can feel the steady beating of your heart
Tearing all my heavy walls apart

[Chorus]
I am lost in wonder every time you smile
Want to stay and hold you for a little while
You are the gentle fire in the cold of night
Making all my broken pieces feel alright

[Post-Chorus]
Lost in wonder when I look at you
There is nothing else I ever want to do

[Verse 2]
The wooden floor is creaking down the hall
Your shadow dances slowly on the wall
I want to memorize the way you move
And settle deeply in this tender groove

[Pre-Chorus 2]
Every single breath is pulling me so close
You are the only magic that I need the most
I can feel the steady beating of your heart
Tearing all my heavy walls apart

[Chorus]
I am lost in wonder every time you smile
Want to stay and hold you for a little while
You are the gentle fire in the cold of night
Making all my broken pieces feel alright

[Post-Chorus 2]
Lost in wonder when I look at you
There is nothing else I ever want to do

[Bridge]
I used to wander through the quiet, fading gloom
But now Saeed has found his heaven in this room
I want to keep this feeling safe and warm
Our little shelter from the winter storm

[Spoken]
[Whispered]
(I just want to lose myself in your eyes)
(Forever right here beneath these open skies)

[Final Chorus]
I am lost in wonder every time you smile
Want to stay and hold you for a little while
You are the gentle fire in the cold of night
Making all my broken pieces feel alright
